Sheep Game

---------------

On a quaint farm in Metaverse, a group of sheep and wolves gather together, squeeze in a farm to cultivate, and automatically collect the crops generated by the $SHEEP Token transaction. With more sheep and wolves, more profits can be earn.

Sheep Game is a NFT risk protocol with novel token economics. It shows the possibility of interaction between ERC-20 and ERC-721 protocols. This is the first time that your SHEEP NFT can win you $BUSD. The more NFTs you have, the more $BUSD you will earn. Sheep Game created a new type of NFT mechanism. Completely decentralized. There are no road maps or empty promises. It's just a game in Metaverse that can be played at launch.

---------------

  • There will only ever be 10,000 SHEEP NFT, minted for 0.2 BNB each.
  • SHEEP Token can be traded on PancakeSwap, and 5% of the friction fee in the transaction is reflected as $BUSD injected into the farm
  • SHEEP NFT can be mortgaged on the farm to earn $BUSD in the farm
  • If SHEEP NFT is un-staked from the farm, there will be no more $BUSD harvest
  • in the $SHEEP transaction 8% Hold Farm, 5% NFT Farm, 2% Deflation,
